    Orphanage

    The Saint Francis Boys' Home, located in Boston, Massachusetts, was the orphanage in which Sam and Nathan Drake (then Morgan) were left following their mother's suicide. It was staffed by nuns. Nathan Drake was placed in the orphanage aged five after his father surrendered him to the state. Sam left the orphanage when he was old enough to find a job to provide for the two. As he grew up, young Nate developed a fascination with British explorer and privateer Sir Francis Drake.
